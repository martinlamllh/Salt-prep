⚗️ Salt Preparation Master

Welcome! This interactive game is designed to help you practise and master the chemical laboratory methods used to prepare various salts.

📖 How to Play

  • Easy Mode: Prepare a target salt from scratch in a single step.
  • Advanced Mode: Determine if a specific synthesis requires 1 or 2 steps based on a given starting material and target salt.
  • The game consists of 10 rounds/missions.
  • You have a maximum of two attempts per question:
    • Correct on 1st attempt: 10 marks
    • Correct on 2nd attempt: 5 marks
    • Incorrect on both attempts: 0 marks

⚠ Please enter your name to start the game!
