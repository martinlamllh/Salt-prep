⚗️ Salt Preparation Master

Welcome! This interactive game is designed to help you practise and master the chemical laboratory methods used to prepare various soluble and insoluble salts.

📖 How to Play

  • The game consists of 10 rounds, covering all three major preparation methods (Titration, Precipitation, and Acid + Insoluble Reactant).
  • Your task is to drag and drop the correct words from the option bank into the five empty slots to build a valid reaction equation and select the correct method.
  • You have a maximum of two attempts per question:
    • Correct on 1st attempt: 10 marks
    • Correct on 2nd attempt: 5 marks
    • Incorrect on both attempts: 0 marks
  • If your answer is incorrect on the first try, your words will remain in the boxes so you can easily amend them. Read the feedback below the boxes to understand your mistake!
